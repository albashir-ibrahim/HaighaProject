About Haighatech

HAIGHA TECH provides one stop automated solution for your trade and industry. Depending on the size and field of your organization, we have different products and services to meet your requirements. We provide the optimum and customized solutions made for your organization.

HAIGHA TECH began its business operation as a hardware and software solutions providing company in January 2017. HAIGHA TECH is focusing exclusively in high quality and cost-effective software development and implementation of services. We are advancing on a tremendous pace and with involvement of skilled and experienced people working in the organization. HAIGHA TECH is currently doing business in Government, iNGOs, Large Corporate, and Schools.
